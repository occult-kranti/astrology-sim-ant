The Jung chart tool — the charts he actually made

Three kinds of chart-work are documented in Jung's own writing, and this tool computes all three on the site's verified engine: the psychological horoscope he cast in difficult diagnoses ("a further point of view from an entirely different angle" — to B. V. Raman, 1947); the synastry cross-aspects his 1952 marriage experiment tallied between spouses' charts; and the aeon clock of Aion — the precession of the vernal point through the fishes.

Honest note — please read Jung used astrology as an interpretive, projective instrument; he never validated it. His own experiment showed nothing beyond chance and he said so. The astronomy below is real and verifiable; the archetypal readings are a historical school of interpretation (Jung's, and where flagged, his followers') — described for study, never a diagnosis, a prediction, or advice.
