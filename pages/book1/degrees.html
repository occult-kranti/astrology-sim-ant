3 · The body-parts grid — planet by sign

Lilly's rule: a planet in its own sign governs the Head; in the next sign the Neck; then Arms, Breast, Heart, Belly, Reins, Secrets, Thighs, Knees, Legs, Feet — counting from its earliest domicile. (So Saturn in Gemini → the Belly.) Used to locate a disease in the body.
