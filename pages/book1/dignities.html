How essential dignity works

A planet is dignified when it occupies a sign or degree it has rulership over. Lilly recognises five, in descending strength: domicile (+5), exaltation (+4), triplicity (+3), term (+2) and face (+1). A planet in the sign opposite its domicile is in detriment (−5); opposite its exaltation it is in fall (−4). A planet with no dignity of its own at all is peregrine (−5) — “wandering”, like a stranger with no standing.

“A Planet is then said to be really strong when he hath many essentiall dignities… in his house, exaltation, triplicity, terme or face, at time of the erecting of the figure.” — Lilly, Christian Astrology, Book I
