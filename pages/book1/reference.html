Signs, Planets & Houses

Lilly's descriptions, condensed from Book I of Christian Astrology (1647). Jump to: the 12 signs · the 7 planets · the 12 houses.

The seven planets

For each planet Lilly gives its nature, rulerships, the manners it produces when well or ill dignified, the kinds of people and professions it signifies, the diseases it governs, and its metals, colours and associations.

The twelve houses

Lilly follows “the Ptolemaian doctrine” of the houses and rejects the Arabic parts of fortune-style house schemes as untrustworthy.
