The Workbench — every calculation, linked in one tool

One moment and place runs the whole engine at once through a single composed reading (fullReading): the figure, the full dignity ledger, aspects & reception, the lots & antiscia, chart health, horary perfection, the Picatrix election layer, the talisman recipe, and — with a birth moment — the whole life trajectory; plus the 🕉 Vedic (Jagannath Hora) chart side by side (toggle below). Every value is the same engine the dedicated tools use; nothing is re-derived. You can export as JSON/Markdown, share by link, save & publish the reading, download the chart, and ask the AI assistant to explain it (Groq free tier or Claude — your own key).

Where each number comes from: every panel below links to its dedicated tool, its step-by-step explanation, and the glossary — see the Reference index at the foot for the full map of functions & data.

Honest note — please read This is a historical study tool. The astronomy is real and verifiable; the interpretations are those of William Lilly (1647) and the medieval Picatrix, reproduced faithfully so the tradition can be examined — never as guidance or a forecast. Astrology has no demonstrated predictive validity and is regarded by the scientific community as a pseudoscience; the magical/talismanic material is recorded as historical practice only and is never a recommendation. The AI assistant narrates these computed, cited results and is held to the same framing. See Sources & Science.
