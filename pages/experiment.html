Test it yourself — the falsification demo

Astrology's central claim is that the moment encodes the outcome. Here is a way to look at that claim directly: take an aim and a place, permute the moment across a window, and watch how the “fitness” score is distributed over random times — the null distribution. See where your chosen moment falls in it, and how sensitive the verdict is to a few minutes' change.

Why this matters The numbers are real and reproducible — but a “favourable” verdict is favourable by the rule's arithmetic. Its frequency is set by how strict the rule is, not by any measured effect on events; no controlled study has found electional timing to influence outcomes. This tool shows the rule's behaviour so you can judge it for yourself. See Sources & Science.
