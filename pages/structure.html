Structure & patterns — the hidden symmetry

Whatever one makes of astrology's claims, the system is a small, elegant piece of modular arithmetic and reflection. Here are four of its symmetries, each demonstrated by computation — not as evidence of an influence, but to see clearly how the rules are built.
