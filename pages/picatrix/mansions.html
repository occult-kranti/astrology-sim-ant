The 28 Mansions of the Moon

The tropical lunar mansions: twenty-eight uniform arcs of the ecliptic, each 12°51′26″ (360° ÷ 28) beginning from 0° Aries. These are the mansions used in the Picatrix and in Cornelius Agrippa's Three Books of Occult Philosophy for astrological magic, indexed here by the Moon's ecliptic longitude.

Honest note The mathematics is real and reproducible — the Moon's longitude comes from a verified astronomical engine (≈1 arc-minute), and each mansion is a plain division of the zodiac. The magical uses are the historical interpretations of Agrippa and the Picatrix, presented strictly for historical study — astrology has no demonstrated predictive validity. See Sources & Science.
