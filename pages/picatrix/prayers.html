Planetary Prayers & Spirits — Picatrix Books III & IV

The Picatrix's third and fourth books gather the seven planetary prayers (III.7), the planetary spirits of the Liber Antimaquis (III.9), the Perfect Nature — the practitioner's personal celestial genius (III.6) — and Book IV's suffumigations and natural magic. They are presented here as historical text for study, the doctrine of a tradition — described, never prescribed.

These link to the calculators: the planet ruling an election or talisman is the one whose prayer and spirit the tradition would address — and the Workbench assistant can recite or explain a planet's prayer on request.
