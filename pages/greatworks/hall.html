Manly P. Hall — The Secret Teachings of All Ages

Hall's 1928 encyclopedia is the site's single richest source-map: forty-five chapters of Masonic, Hermetic, Qabbalistic and Rosicrucian symbolism, each pointing at a Workbench engine that reproduces its calculation — the Tree of Life for the Qabbalah chapters, the tarot for chapter 28, the Picatrix correspondences for the plant/stone/metal chapters, the alchemy page for the Great Work. The 1928 text is public domain twice over; where Hall retails legend (Atlantis, the Baconian thesis) it is flagged as his claim.
