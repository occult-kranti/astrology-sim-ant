The Kindred — Lévi · Ficino · Iamblichus · Regardie

Four authors who shape what the site's esoteric tools encode. Éliphas Lévi printed the influential tarot–Hebrew-letter key behind the Tree's levi-fool dispute; Marsilio Ficino supplied the astrological-medicine and planetary-music layer (and has no quotable English translation); Iamblichus is the philosophical defense of theurgy and a late-antique taxonomy of divination to set beside the site's four oracles; and Israel Regardie printed the Golden Dawn corpus the site cites — cite-only, because its US renewal status could not be verified. Described, never prescribed; every contested claim flagged.
