Aleister Crowley — the system of "Magick"

Crowley printed the Golden Dawn's once-secret correspondence tables as 777 — whose Key Scale rows are the rows of the site's Tree of Life — founded the religion of Thelema on Liber AL, whose verse I:57 is the exact source of the site's tzaddi–heh dispute, and organized the whole as Book 4 / Magick. Public-domain status is per work: the pre-1930 works are quote-safe; the 1944 Book of Thoth is cite-only until 2040. Every efficacy claim is Crowley's claim; the rituals are catalogued with their publication history in place of instructions.
