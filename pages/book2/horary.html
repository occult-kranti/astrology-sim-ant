Horary Chart Calculator

A horary chart is cast for the moment a question is understood. This tool erects the figure in Lilly's Regiomontanus houses, finds the significators, scores every planet's dignity, lists the aspects by Lilly's orbs, and runs the considerations before judgement.

Tip: choose the house that signifies your question — e.g. marriage & theft are the 7th, money the 2nd, career the 10th, sickness the 6th. The full guide is in Book II, house by house.
