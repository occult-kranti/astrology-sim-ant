The Questions, House by House

Book II is organised by the twelve houses. To judge a question, first decide which house rules the matter — that house and its lord become the quesited. Here is Lilly's arrangement, with the kinds of question he files under each house.

Turning the chart To ask about a third party, make that person's house a new Ascendant and count the others from it. Your brother's money is the 2nd from the 3rd; a friend's illness is the 6th from the 11th.
